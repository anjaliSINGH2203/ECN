GOAL OF ECN

We serve as a centralized hub where users can request vital supplies such as food, water, and medical aid during emergencies. Collaborating closely with various NGOs and humanitarian organizations, the site streamlines the process of resource allocation, ensuring that help reaches those in need quickly and effectively

      A cloudburst in South Lhonak Lake triggered a flash flood in Teesta, killing at least 42 people and displacing thousands

    • 2023 landslides in Himachal Pradesh

      Heavy rains caused landslides in several districts, killing over 70 people.

    • 2022 Trikut cable car accident

      A cable-car collision at Trikut Hill in Deoghar district, Jharkhand, India, killed at least three people and injured others.

    • 2023 Andhra Pradesh train collision

      On 29 October 2023, two passenger trains collided in southern India's Vizianagaram district in Andhra Pradesh state killing at least 14 people and leaving about 60 others injured. Three carriages of a stationary passenger train were derailed after it was hit by another train.

    You can offer surplus supplies directly on the platform, creating a community-driven resource-sharing network. This feature allows you to contribute to the relief effort easily

    Feedback and Ratings

    Provide feedback on the assistance received and rate NGOs based on their response effectiveness. This will build trust and accountability within the community
